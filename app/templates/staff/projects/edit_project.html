There are six pieces of information required in a project brief:

  • The Title is a one-line summary of the project used to identify the project easily.
  • The Academic year is selected from a drop-down list.
  • The Overview is a longer description of the project. Students rely on this to form their ideas about what the project involves. This section should include the goals, type of activity and the technology to be used (if known). You should also highlight the attractive features of the project here.
  • The Deliverables section lists the expected results from the project. Here, it is good to be specific: if you are expecting a piece of software that should be clearly stated. Other deliverables could include reports, design documentation, graphical assets, etc.
  • The Resources available is another list, this time detailing what you can provide to the team to help get the work done. This varies enormously from one project to another, and could include software or hardware resources, workspace, documentation, etc. It is also a good idea to be specific about how much of your time the students can expect to benefit from.
  • The Skills required section lets you choose skills from a list. To pick more than one, use CTRL-Click on a PC and Command-Click on a Mac. If you do not have a clear idea of the skills your project may require, just choose something generic and a member of academic staff will be able to suggest something more specific.

Once your project brief has been saved, it will be reviewed by a member of academic staff who will discuss any issues with you. Changes may be needed, for example, if the scope of the project is too broad, or if there is anything that the students may not understand clearly. Staff have extensive experience of preparing instructions for students and are in a good position to help make your project brief accessible.

After your project is submitted, it is not immediately visible to students. This is to allow an opportunity to discuss the details and make any amendments that may be needed.

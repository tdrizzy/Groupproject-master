Welcome to Projects Exchange


This site is operated by the School of Computing at Edinburgh Napier University

Here you can post project briefs for the students completing the Group Project module which takes place between January and April each year.

To get started quickly, just click the New project link in the menu and fill in the boxes.

Please remember to update your company profile information.


Each page of the site has help informaton available which you can access by clicking Help in the menu.

After you have saved a new project, it goes through a checking process before it is visible to the students. The details will be reviewed by a member of academic staff who may discuss some changes with you.

For further informaton, you can use the FAQ link at the foot of the page, or use the Contact link to send us a message.

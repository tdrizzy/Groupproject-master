Privacy


Any data that you provide via this system is stored in a protected database, and is only used for the purposes of managing student projects. This includes:

  • Displaying project information to students
  • Sending you email updates on your posted projects
  • Sending you communications from University staff regarding current and future projects

In addition, the system offers the option to display your email address to students so that they can contact you directly regarding projects that you have posted.

Cookies

Cookies are used buy the site in order to maintain the connection to the server while you are logged in. This is technically known as a session cookie without which the system would not work. Cookies are not used for any other purpose.

Your data will be protected in line with the University's general privacy policy.
